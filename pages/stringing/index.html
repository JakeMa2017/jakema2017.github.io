Q: How much for stringing a racket

    A: $15/racket. $10 if you bring your own string.

    A: On average, it takes 2-3 days to get one racket done. Please see status tab for the most updated time.


Q: What does this "Flexible Pick Up Time" mean?

    A: I usually play on Mon, Wed and Fri afternoon from 5:30 - 7:00. You can come to the court during these time to get your racket.
